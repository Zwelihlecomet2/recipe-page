Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omolette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optinally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillimgs:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats,herbs
